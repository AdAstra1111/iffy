The Problem

There are two problems in AI filmmaking.

Problem 1: AI video

Almost good enough. But a great-looking video of a bad scene is still a bad scene.

Problem 2: AI can't write

IFFY solves both.

The Insight

Hallucination isn't the bug.
It's the thing that makes characters human.

Human beings hallucinate themselves — partially invented backstories, constantly rewritten. That internal fiction is the source of subtext. A character without it isn't a person.

We use hallucination to create psychological depth that no script outline can specify.
