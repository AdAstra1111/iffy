Past Lives

82

Nora and Hae Sung, two deeply connected childhood friends, are forced apart after Nora's family emigrates from South Korea. Twenty years later, they are reunited in New York for one fateful week.

Nora Young is a playwright on the verge of her biggest breakthrough. Her marriage to Arthur is loving but has settled into quiet companionship. When Hae Sung — her childhood first love — contacts her, she agrees to meet him in New York.

Over seven days, Nora finds herself torn between the life she has built and the life she left behind. Each meeting peels back another layer of their shared history and unexplored feelings.
