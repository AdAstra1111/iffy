Treatment

Past Lives

82

Nora and Hae Sung, two deeply connected childhood friends, are forced apart after Nora's family emigrates from South Korea. Twenty years later, they are reunited in New York for one fateful week that will determine the course of their lives.

Nora Young (born Na Young) is a playwright on the verge of her biggest breakthrough. Her marriage to Arthur, a fellow writer, is loving but has settled into the quiet rhythm of companionship rather than passion. When Hae Sung contacts her, she agrees to meet him during his first visit to New York.

What begins as a polite reunion between old friends quickly deepens. Over seven days, Nora finds herself torn between the life she has built and the life she left behind. Hae Sung, still carrying the memory of their childhood connection, represents a path not taken.
